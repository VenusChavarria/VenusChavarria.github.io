Consumer Products Excel FP&A Financial Modeling Executive Reporting

Executive Summary

This project presents an Executive Monthly FP&A Performance Review developed for a consumer products company operating in a competitive market where sustained growth depends on balancing revenue expansion, profitability, operational efficiency, and product mix.

The review provides management with an integrated view of financial and operational performance by combining budgeting, KPI monitoring, trend analysis, and executive reporting to transform business data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

Business Objective

Provide management with a structured monthly review of business performance by comparing actual results against budget, evaluating financial and operational KPIs, identifying performance drivers, and supporting informed business decisions.
